General Guidelines

AirBind is a property managed by SortMyEntries. We work as official offline ticketing partners for various events.

1. Promote Events

Browse the dashboard to find events you want to promote. Click "Details" to access:

  • Promotional Text: Copy the official promotional text.
  • Flyers: Download high-quality event flyers.
  • Reference Links: Check official event pages for price verification.

2. Booking & Payments

When you get a booking request:

  1. Collect Payment: Receive payment from the client directly to your account or the AirBind account (mentioned below).
  2. Create Order: Use the "Book Now" button on the dashboard.
  3. Enter Details: Provide accurate client details (Name, Email, Mobile) and the Transaction ID for verification.
  4. Verification: AirBind will verify the transaction and approve the bookings.

3. Ticket Delivery

Once the bookings are approved by AirBind:

  • Tickets are punched directly via online booking platforms.
  • Clients receive tickets on their linked email ID or contact number.
  • You will see the order status change to Completed in your dashboard.

4. Get Paid

Commission Payment Process:

  1. Go to the My Orders section in your dashboard.
  2. For "Completed" orders, click the Request Commission button.
  3. AirBind will process the payment to your registered UPI ID.
  4. Once paid, the status will update to "Paid".

Cancellation: AirBind has full rights to cancel a booking depending on ticket availability or other valid reasons.
